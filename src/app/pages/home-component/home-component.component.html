What is I Grow Chicken?

I Grow Chicken develops solutions to support the profitable growing of poultry livestock by making an integrated set of tools available on a smartphone and browser alike we are putting reports and know-how into the hands of Integrations or Individual Farmers.

The scalable nature of the solution allows for a wide range in size. All in all, we currently serve around 800 farmers. The I Grow Chicken Solution is a fully integrated solution covering the full value chain of the poultry industry. The design of the solution facilitates managing data for own operations as well as out growers.
