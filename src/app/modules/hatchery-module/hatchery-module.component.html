The Hatchery

Our software helps the hatchery manager manage the entire 21-day lifecycle of hatchery operations, from setting to hatching. There's no need for calculations or calendars for your hatchery manager.

I Grow Chicken provides a comprehensive egg placement planning report and monitors the flock from breeder to hatchery. IGC's hatchery module enhances your production quality by offering features such as breakopen analysis, chick grading, and much more.

Features

Realtime Monitoring & Tracking

Track and monitor your hatch plan, process data, dispatch and sales with an intuitive and simple to interprete dashboard.

Gain in-depth insights into hatchery performance with detailed summaries, productivity metrics and outcomes. Get data on hatch & mortality rates, chick quality, production percentages, and other key performance indicators, enabling informed decision-making and strategic planning.

Customize Hatchery Controls

I Grow Chicken's ERP allows hatchery managers to control the hatchery controls such as temperature, humidity, and turning cycles among many other features thus allowing for effective egg incubation and hatching.

With this advanced hatchery features, hatchery managers can easily manage the entire hatchery process from start to finish ensuring maximum hatch rates and healthy production of chicks.

Hatch Transfer

I Grow Chicken facilitates seamless hatching process management by enabling users to monitor hatch rates and efficiently transfer newly hatched chicks to the appropriate facilities. This functionality optimizes post-incubation operations, boosts hatchery efficiency, and ensures the safe and proper care of chicks.
