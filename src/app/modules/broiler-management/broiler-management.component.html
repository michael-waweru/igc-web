Broiler Management

Enhance your production capabilities with I Grow Chicken's Broiler Farm Module. Monitor flock performance, feed conversion and various other metrics to make well-informed decisions.

Effectively Plan, do and Manage

Our software provides an efficient way to plan and manage your broiler flock. By utilizing our software, you can monitor growth rates, monitor feed consumption, and analyze health data, ensuring optimal performance. This allows you to informed decisions quickly and efficiently, analyzing historical data to allow you get the best yield out of your flock.

Remote Access

No matter where your farm is located, you can enter data using a mobile app. Our mobile app saves data locally until you have internet access, allowing it to sync with the system and ensure seamless operations, even in remote areas with unreliable network connectivity.

Data Management

Ensure the well-being of the chicks by collecting essential data such as temperature, humidity, water intake, feed intake, and many other details. I Grow Chicken's broiler farm module facilitates data capture and provide access to historical data, making it easier for you to analyze information and make informed decisions.
