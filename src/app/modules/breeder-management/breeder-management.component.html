Breeder Management

Our breeder module covers the entire breeder life cycle, including placement, feeding, egg collection, and hatchery operations, with purchasing, sales, inventory, and accounting. Capture daily farm data on your smartphone monitor health and performance with reports and enhance efficiency and profits. Effective management leads to lower costs and improved yields through better data capture and forecasting.

Feed & Water Management

Effectively oversee feed inventory for your poultry farm using our tailored system. Simplify the ordering, tracking, and management feed supplies to provide your birds with optimal nutrition. Implement automated reordering-based consumption patterns. Improve cost efficiency, reduce waste, and ensure a steady feed supply for healthy and productive poultry operations.

Disease Management

Safeguard your flock from diseases. Our software allows you to track mortality, collect samples, and document reasons, empowering your veterinarian to conduct precise post-mortem analyses and recommend effective remedies.

Comprehensive Reports

Leverage customizable reports to uncover valuable insights into your flock's performance and productivity. Evaluate essential metrics like growth rates, mortality rates, feed conversion ratios, and production costs to pinpoint areas for improvement, execute targeted interventions, and overall flock health and profitability. Make informed decisions, monitor progress over time, and attain sustainable success in poultry farming with data-driven, flock-specific reports.
