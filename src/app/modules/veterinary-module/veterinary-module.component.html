Veterinary Management

The veterinary module is designed to ensuring the health and well-being of the birds. It helps in the prevention and control of diseases, which can significantly impact productivity and profitability.

This module includes features such as medication schedule, alert and notification, and medication cost included to bird cost. Overall, this module will help farmers to keep track of medication, reduce the risk of disease outbreak and improve the bird's health.

Core Features

Schedule Medication Easily

Maintain your birds' health effortlessly by utilizing the medication scheduling feature in I Grow Chicken Software. This tool ensures timely and consistent administration of medications, which is vital for disease prevention and overall well-being. With a proper schedule in place, your birds will receive the treatments they need to thrive.

Get detailed Health Reports

Get informed and detailed flock health from the I Grow Chicken Software. Gain valuable insights on how vaccination is administered, who administered the medication, whether the proper medication schedules are being adhered to and much more. This information will help you make informed decisions and improve your bird's health, identify potential issues and take necessary actions.

Flexibility & Customization

With our software, we allow you to customize your medication schedule to fit your specific needs. You can add or remove disease types and adjust mortality reasons, to appear visible in your postmoterm resports to give you custom tailored experience and avoid cluttered and unrealistic data for your farm and flock.
