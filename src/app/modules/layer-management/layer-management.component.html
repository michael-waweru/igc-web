Layer Management

IGC empowers layer farms to make informed, data-driven decisions, boosting efficiency and ensuring a competitive edge in the market. By adopting IGC, farms can optimize flock management, enhance egg quality, and achieve greater success in their operations.

Streamlined planning and feeding schedules

I Grow Chicken helps layer farms to efficiently organize and undertake essential tasks like feeding, egg collection, and weight taking, ensuring seamless and effective operations. Tracks feed consumption on a daily & weekly basis for flocks, enabling adjustments to rations and ensuring optimal growth.

Achieve optimal egg quality

Logs the daily egg collection count, including quality evaluations and irregularities. Classifies eggs based on size and grade, offering valuable insights into quality and market potential. Monitors occurrences of broken or damaged eggs, highlighting possible concerns with handling or laying practices.

Accurate and Detailed Reports

Get comprehensive performance & Growth reports, empowering layer farms to analyze key metrics and make informed decisions whilst identifying areas for improvement. IGC will help determine feed efficiency by analyzing feed intake per unit of egg production, enabling better feed management, cost reduction and maximum yields.
